CHASMIDA

Main Purpose

The State Governor appointed Prince Ntui Ogar as the Director-General (DG) of the Agency. To expedite decision-making and improve efficiency, the DG reports directly to the State Governor, eliminating bureaucratic delays that could hinder the Agency’s effectiveness. The State Governor also approved the establishment of Cross-Page Energy Ltd, CPEL, a collaborative effort involving WPIC, investors, and Cross River State which serves as the project operator, with the Agency representing Cross River State on CPEL’s board. The major projects encompass hydrocarbon exploration and production, the construction of modular refineries, an LNG plant, and a Natural Gas Processing complex for the production of LPG, CNG, NGL, and other marketable natural gas liquids. Additionally, there are plans to establish processing plants for solid minerals. The realization of these projects would be unprecedented in the country, marking the first time a Nigerian State Government collaborates with private investors to establish a gas processing complex and an LNG plant. Leveraging Calabar’s strategic location as a Port City and its Free Trade Zone designation, we intend to maximize LNG and processed solid minerals for export, utilizing the NNPC Jetty. With Calabar as the hub, we will purchase raw minerals from other states for storage, processing, and export.

Environmental Sustainability

Mining activities have the potential to impact the environment adversely. CHASMIDA imposes stringent environmental regulations on mining companies to mitigate these risks. These regulations include guidelines for land reclamation, waste disposal, and the reduction of air and water pollution. By adhering to these standards, mining operations can coexist harmoniously with the surrounding ecosystems.

Community Engagement

Mining should be a source of prosperity for local communities. CHASMIDA encourages mining companies to engage with these communities through corporate social responsibility initiatives.These efforts can include the construction of schools, healthcare facilities, and job creation programs.Additionally, fair compensation and employment opportunities should be provided to the local workforce.

Sustainable Energy Initiatives

Cross River State has also embarked on ambitious initiatives aimed at harnessing clean and renewable energy sources.The collaboration with NNPC and NUPRC extends to these efforts, with a shared goal of diversifying the energy portfolio and reducing environmental impact.Solar, wind, and biomass projects are being explored, aligning with the national drive toward sustainable energy sources.
